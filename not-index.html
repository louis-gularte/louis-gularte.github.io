Welcome to my website!

When I'm being academic, I can most often be found in the philosophy department and the Cognitive, Linguistic, and Psychological Sciences department at Brown University.

My research – mostly in philosophy of mind, ethics & metaethics, and cognitive/affective neuroscience – tends to center on issues related to negative emotion(s), and suffering in particular.

I'm also currently a lecturer in the Humanities, Philosophy, and Social Sciences (HPSS) department at Rhode Island School of Design (RISD). (See my teaching page.)
